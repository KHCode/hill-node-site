About

Like everyone else, this past year has been one hell of a ride for my family and I. For me personally, this was one wild cap to a string of challenging years. Some of this was self-imposed, as I chose to go back to school to get my bachelor's degree in computer science while raising a toddler and supporting my partner as she worked on her PhD.

But I finished 2020 with my degree and am now excited to be looking for full-time work as a software developer and/or designer! I have experience working with both compiled languages and interpreted languages, both declarative and imperative programming, object-oriented programming, and component-driven programming. I love to unpack difficult problems, design user interactions, and collaborate with others to solve important problems.
